ℹ️ Drive$Tracking v3.0

Your all-in-one delivery driver finance app. Track income, expenses, mileage, investments, appointments, and notes — all saved locally on your device. Scan receipts with AI. Download and share reports.

📊 Import Preview
❓ How to Copy from Google Sheets
Step 1 — Open your Google Sheet on your phone or computer
Step 2 — Tap the top-left corner cell (A1) to start selecting
Step 3 — Select ALL your data rows including the header row
Step 4 — Copy (Ctrl+C or long press → Copy)
Step 5 — Come back here and paste into the text box
Step 6 — Tap Preview Import and check everything looks right
💡 Tips
• Your columns can be in any order — the app figures it out
• Dates can be MM/DD/YYYY, YYYY-MM-DD, or Month DD YYYY
• Source column (DoorDash, UberEats etc) = Income entry
• Category column (Gas, Food etc) = Expense entry
• Only miles with no amount = Mileage entry
• Rows with problems are shown as warnings before import
